RC4 Cipher (Rivest Cipher 4)

RC4 is a stream cipher. While historically popular for its simplicity and speed, it is now considered insecure for new applications due to known vulnerabilities. It uses a variable-length key (typically 40-256 bits) to generate a pseudo-random keystream, which is then XORed with the plaintext.
